Mobile First Auto Scroll

Section 1

This page is built mobile first. The controls are touch-friendly and optimized for small screens.

Section 2

Use Start, Pause, Stop, direction, speed, and manual scroll buttons.

Section 3

The speed slider controls how often the page scrolls. Lower value means faster movement.

Section 4

The step selector controls how many pixels the page moves each scroll tick.

Section 5

You can scroll down automatically, scroll up automatically, or manually jump to top.

Section 6

This is useful for mobile presentations, kiosk screens, article previews, or dashboards.

Section 7

End of content. Auto-scroll stops automatically at the bottom or top.
