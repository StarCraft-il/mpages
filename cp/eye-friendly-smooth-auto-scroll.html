Eye Friendly Smooth Auto Scroll

Scrolling happens inside the smooth reading window, with soft top/bottom fade to reduce flicker.

Ready. Choose Start for very smooth requestAnimationFrame scrolling.

Section 1

This version scrolls only the reading window instead of the whole page. That keeps the controls stable and makes the motion easier on the eye.

Section 2

The movement uses requestAnimationFrame and time-based pixels-per-second scrolling. This usually feels smoother than timer-based scrolling.

Section 3

You can control the visible area size. A smaller area is good for focused reading, while a larger area is better for presentation or dashboard mode.

Section 4

The top and bottom fade masks help hide sharp movement at the edges and reduce the feeling of flicker.

Section 5

Use Gentle for slow comfortable motion, Reading for normal text, and Faster only when you want a quick preview.

Section 6

Manual Up and Down buttons move in soft animated steps. Touching or using the mouse wheel pauses the automatic scroll.

Section 7

The page avoids layout changes during scrolling, uses a fixed scroll container, and keeps animation calculations very small.

Section 8

End of content. Auto-scroll pauses automatically when it reaches the bottom or the top.
